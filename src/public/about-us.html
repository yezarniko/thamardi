Welcome to

Thamardi Private High School

Thamardi Private High School welcomes all students and their families, celebrates diversity and promotes relationships built on mutual respect. We believe everyone has the capacity to be creative. Thamardi is a place where people use design to develop their own creative potential.

Mission

Our mission is to change the way education meets the future; to foster interdisciplinary, integrated thinking and innovative leadership; to engage fully in the global community; and to facilitate lifelong learning.

Vision

Our school will foster an environment to nurture individuals academically, socially, and emotionally so that they are equipped to tackle academic challenges and become productive members of society.

LeaderinMe

It is based on principles and practices of personal, interpersonal and organizational effectiveness, and upon the powerful premise that every child possesses unique strengths and has the ability to be a leader.

The History of Thamardi

Thamardi private hostel was opened in the year 1955-56. There was only grade 9 and 10 when it was started to open. The founders of Thamadi hostel are U Sein Win (B.sc, Dip.Ed) and Daw Kyi Kyi Myint (B.Ed, M.Ed(Methodology) Phillippines). In 2013-14, Meiktila Thamardi private hostel was upgraded to private high school.
 In 2019-20, Thamardi Private High School (Mawlamyine) was opened by the son of U Sein Win and Daw Kyi Kyi Myint, U Myo Thawtar Win (B.Tech(Electronic Engineering) NUS(Singapore)) and his wife, Daw Hnin Yee Lwin (B.A(English), DIM(Yangon University of Economics)) as a branch of Thamardi Private High School (Meiktila).
Currently, Daw Mya Win (B.A, B.Ed) is working as the headmistress of Thamardi Private High School (Mawlamyine).
