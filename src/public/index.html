Our school, Thamardi is a LeaderinMe school. LeaderinMe is a process that teaches students not to lead others but to lead themselves. Learning LeaderinMe can make students stand up by themselves. As the LeaderinMe environment expands, our environment imporves. Thamardi school will try to change not only the education but also the environment.

There are class tutors in every classroom to take care of the students. When teachers attend the meeting, they look after them not to be fought and be noisy.In Thamardi School, there are personal writing desks for the students to learn well and comfortably. There are only around 20 students per room. So the teachers can teach lessons systematically and carefully. All rooms have 24-hour electricity and air conditioners. So students can study and learn in peace.

Bordering

Thamardi private shcool has a hostel system for the convenience of remote students.In the hostel, there are three meals each day for feeding hostel students.And they prepare systematically to study and review the lessons with the guides(study) The purpose of having a hostel is to teach the students to be a disciplined systematic and orderly manner students.The next intention is to avoid, by heart, and promote self-study. If you want to know anything about the hostel, please contact us.
